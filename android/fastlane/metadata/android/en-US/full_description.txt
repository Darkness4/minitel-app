Minitel Toolbox offers a suite of tools to facilitate Internet access at the École des Mines de St-Etienne, on our dear George Charpak Campus.

➤ Features and functions

•   Authentication to Stormshield/Portal/Gateway + automatic connection to the EMSE Portal
•   Facebook news and application changelog
•   Agenda with adjustable notifications
•   Quick access to Sogo, EMSE GCP Printer and Minitel Wiki.
•   Quick reporting to Minitel with full diagnosis.
•   Documentation of the network and diagnosis included in the application.
•   Documentation of the printer configuration, import of emails, Dual Boot and VM included in the application.
